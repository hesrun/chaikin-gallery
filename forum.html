KONSTANTIN CHAYKIN TOOK PART IN THE ST. PETERSBURG INTERNATIONAL ECONOMIC FORUM

  • BACK TO LIST BACK TO LIST
Russian watchmaker and inventor Konstantin Chaykin has for the first time taken part in the St. Petersburg International Economic Forum as an official speaker.

On June 14 the St. Petersburg International Economic Forum opened its doors, as it has done every year since 1997. Among the participants and the guests of the Forum are heads of states, political leaders, ministers, owners and directors of the largest Russian and foreign businesses, entrepreneurs and artists. In 2022 the Forum was attended by around 14 thousand people from 130 countries

Konstantin Chaykin participated in the discussion "Industrial Design – Foundation of Sovereignty and Flagship of Industry", which took place on June 15 at the Russian Design Space. Russian industrial design is rooted in a centuries-old tradition and is distinguished by a distinct visual language. Industrial designers not only create new looks for Russian-made products, but shape the cultural and technological sovereignty of the country.

The participants have discussed a number of key aspects of Russian industrial design. Konstantin Chaykin has shared his experience in creating a brand and bringing it to the international market.

Chaykin has shared his experience in creating a brand and bringing it to the international market "Before Perestroika, there were 19 watch factories in Russia, making tens of millions of watches each year. By the early 2000s, when I established my manufacture, almost all of them have disappeared or gone bankrupt. Today, 20 years later, there are several full-cycle production facilities, capable of producing not just the case or dial, but movements of their own. But only two of them, ‘Konstantin Chaykin’ and ‘Raketa’, currently have international presence.
